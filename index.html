About

More than a
project manager.

I'm Colleen Murphy, a Project Manager and business support specialist based in Omagh, Northern Ireland, with over 10 years of experience delivering projects across construction, commercial and governance environments.

I work directly with business owners and directors to bring structure, clarity and control to the operational and financial side of their business. Whether that's getting costs under control, building reporting systems, or cutting through the day-to-day chaos. I work practically and efficiently to make a real difference.

As co-director of View2Build, I manage invoicing, budgeting and cost reporting on live construction projects. That's not background knowledge. It's what I do every day.

Services are flexible and tailored to what you actually need. No long-term contracts, no jargon. Just hands-on support that works.
